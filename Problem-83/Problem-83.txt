DAILY CODING PROBLEM - #83
--------------------------
This problem was asked by LinkedIn.

Determine whether a tree is a valid binary search tree.

A binary search tree is a tree with two children, left and right, and satisfies the constraint that the key in the 
left child must be less than or equal to the root and the key in the right child must be greater than or 
equal to the root.

Sample Input:                                                               Sample Output:
5                                                                           true
4 2 5 1 3

Sample Input:                                                               Sample Output:
5                                                                           false
3 2 5 1 4
